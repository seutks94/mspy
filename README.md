## The Elephant in the Room: Is This Even Legal?

Let's address this head-on because it matters.

**Legal uses of mSpy:**
- Monitoring your minor children (under 18)
- Monitoring adults who have given explicit consent
- Monitoring devices you own for legitimate security purposes

**Illegal uses:**
- Spying on adults without their knowledge or consent
- Tracking your partner/spouse without permission
- Monitoring employees without proper disclosure and consent

Laws vary by country and even by state in the US, so check your local regulations before installing. mSpy makes you agree during purchase that you'll only use it legally, but that's your responsibility to ensure.

The company has faced criticism for being marketed as "stalkerware" in some media coverage, particularly when people use it to spy on partners. They've added security measures to reduce misuse, but ultimately, the ethical burden falls on the user.
